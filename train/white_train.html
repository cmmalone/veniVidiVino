White Wine Descriptions

Australian Chardonnay

Gold in the glass, this supple, elegant wine boasts rich aromas of honey and tropical fruits while apples, melon and vanilla oak tantalize the palate. Golden in hue and slightly tart, crisp and rich in varietal character. With aging you get complexity and smoothness.

Comparable Wines: Semillon Chardonnay

Pairing: Great when served with pastas, seafood and shellfish.

Oak: Heavy   Body: Full   Aging: 3-6 months

Available In : Concentrate, Supreme 100% Juice


Bianco Do Trapani

A rich full-bodied white made in the Sicilian tradition. High alcohol is its trademark, with a deep golden color. The nose unveils a wonderful harmony of ripe pears and bananas. To the palate the sensation is warm with rich, soft tannins and an extremely long finish. Not a wine for weak taste buds.

Comparable Wines: None

Pairings: grilled fish, white sauce pastas and grilled veal dishes

Oak: Light   Body: Heavy   Aging: 1-3 months  Sweetness: Dry

Available In : Vintner's Reserve


Brise De Mer

A delicate aromatic French style wine with a soft leafy bouquet. Dry, light and crisp. Its refreshing light and mellow flavor is best enjoyed young.  Very popular with those who enjoy Entre-Deux-Mers wines.

Comparable Wines:  Sauvignon Blanc, White Bordailles

Pairing: Enjoy with summer salads and fruit.

Oak: None   Body: Light   Aging: 1-3 months

Available In: Concentrate


Chamblaise

This easy drinking wine is light-bodied with a clear, smooth refreshing flavor. Essentially dry, but with floral character and a full intensity  of fruit make it a universally appealing blend. One of the most useful wines.

Comparable Wines:  Chardonnay

Pairing: Matches with a variety of fish and seafood.

Oak: None   Body: Light   Aging:   1-3 months

Available In : Concentrate, Supreme 100% Juice


Chardonnay (French)

A very dry, yet full-bodied wine, with gentle oak influenced aroma. This wine is crisp and clean making it wonderfully refreshing. Pleasant on the palate with hints of honey, apples and pears, this is a wine to savor.

Comparable Wines:  Chamblaise, Pinot Chardonnay

Pairing: Serve chilled with fish and seafood.

Oak: Light   Body: Full   Aging: 3-6 months

Available In : Concentrate, Supreme 100% Juice


Chenin Blanc

It's a distinctive dry white wine, can be sweet with  a lush fruity  bouquet. Complements the flavor of apples, cinnamon and honey. Great to age.

Comparable Wines:  None

Pairing: Perfect to serve with salads, cheeses and light pasta dishes.

Oak: None   Body: Light   Aging: 1-3 months

Available In : Concentrate, and Supreme 100% Juice


Cuvee Speciale

Light gold in color, having the intense aromas of citrus and the subtle tangy essence. Full and generous, this is a sumptuous wine with a rather soft finish that is well structured, tickling the palate. Excellent for sipping, perfect for a special meal or alone as a celebrator.

Comparable Wines:  Manoir Blanc

Pairing: Shellfish and salads.

Oak: None   Body: Light   Aging: 1-3 months

Available In : Concentrate and Supreme 100% Juice


French Colombard

Generally light in color, its is crisp yet moderately dry and has a lovely fragrant  fruit bouquet, which make it best to be consumed young and well chilled.  A tingle of acidity carries the wine to a long finish.

Comparable Wines:  Riesling

Pairing: A favorite summer refresher and companion to fish and poultry.

Oak: None   Body: Medium   Aging: 1-3 months

Available In : Concentrate, and Supreme 100% Juice


Gewurztraminer

Deep yellow, it has a floral and lemon citrus nose. It is a lively wine with its trademark spiciness, it offers a frontal attack on your taste buds.  Well-aged it becomes a truly distinctive and pleasant wine.

Comparable Wines:  French Colombard, Riesling

Pairing: Spicy Indian & Thai food, white meats and old cheeses.

Oak: None   Body: Medium   Aging: 3-6 months

Available In : Concentrate, and Supreme 100% Juice


Johannesburg Riesling

A light-colored, delicately flavored wine which offers a slight tartness. Displays a flowery bouquet, complex fruit and cinnamon flavors make it crisp, clean and well structured.

Comparable Wines:  Liebfraumilch

Pairing: Uniquely suited to freshwater fish and other seafood.

Oak: None   Body: Medium   Aging: 1-3 months

Available In : Concentrate, and Supreme 100% Juice


Liebfraumilch

With a pronounced floral bouquet and a perfect blend of sweetness, this is a  particularly good wine when consumed young. Light and elegant, its flavor is soft in the mouth.

Comparable Wines:  Gewuztraminer, French Colombard, Johannisberg Riesling

Pairing: Well with seafood, veal and fruit.

Oak: None   Body: Medium   Aging: 1-3 months

Available In : Concentrate, and Supreme 100% Juice


Luna Di Miele (Honeymoon)

A smooth luscious wine, it is almost liquorous. This wine has a bouquet of honey and apple blossoms. Full on the palate with a lingering finish. Its generous alcohol content will surprise the taste buds with a medley of citrus and herbs.

Comparable Wines: None

Pairings: Sushi, grilled seafoods and rich creamy sauce pastas.

Oak: Light   Body: Medium   Aging: 1-3 months  Sweetness: Dry

Available In : Vintner's Reserve


Manoir Blanc

A yellow wine with delicate zesty aromas. It has well balanced alcohol with citrus flavors and is smooth on the palate. Overall it is a very fresh wine with a well rounded mouth-feel.

Comparable Wines:  Cuvee Speciale

Pairing: Easy drinking, perfect for hors d'oeuvres and light meals.

Oak: None   Body: Medium   Aging: 1-3 months

Pairing: Well with mild cheeses and chicken dishes.

Semillon Chardonnay

This wonderful blend brings together two very different elements in a harmonic union. The Chardonnay brings its flavors of tropical fruits with hints of vanilla, while the Semillon contributes a honeyed vegetal character with nutty butterscotch nuances. The resulting wine is truly a symphony of flavors.

Comparable Wines:  Chardonnay

Pairing: Great with pastas and shellfish.

Oak: Light   Body: Medium   Aging: 3-6 months

Available In : Concentrate and Supreme 100% Juice
